Death and Reincarnation

Book Design, 2022.

From life to death, and from death to life.

It only takes about a week for bananas I bought from supermarkets to decay. They gradually turn from yellow to brown, then to black. They end up as waste, which will eventually be absorbed by the land. Everything goes from life to death. Our body would decay, and be broken by microbes. We are becoming a part of the earth, on which new lives are born. Our life continues in other forms. "Life" and "death" is merely ust an ephemeral state that we experience.
