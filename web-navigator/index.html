Chrome Extension, 2023.

We jump from one website to another by browsing on search engines, or by clicking on links in relative websites. If the internet is a world, what will be the map of it? What do these websites look like if they are houses, gardens or rivers that share the same ground?

"My website is a shifting house next to a river of knowledge. What could yours be?"

This extension visualizes your browsing activity over the past 7 days by transforming each website you visit into a square displayed on a popup window. The size of each square dynamically adjusts based on how frequently you visit a particular website, with larger squares representing more visits. Additionally, the color of each square is determined by the website’s domain name, creating a unique, visually engaging pattern that reflects your browsing habits. This intuitive visualization helps users easily track their web usage and discover trends in their online behavior.
